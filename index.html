Travel to Taiwan

That is why EVERY YEAR..
MILLIONS OF ASIAN PEOPLE TRAVEL TO TAIWAN FOR TAIWANESE FOOD!!!

EVEN THOUGH THEIR OWN FOOD IS ALREADY CONSIDERED TASTY TO THE WORLD.

And that, it certainly INCLUDES CHINESE PEOPLE COMING FROM MAINLAND CHINA...

THEY ALL CHOOSE TO COME TO TAIWAN FOR CHINESE FOOD...
